Friendship Dairies - Cottage Confessions

Brief


Create a targeted 10-15 second sharable social/digital campaign video for the Friendship Dairies Cottage Cheese to generate excitement for the brand.


Final Product



Inspiration


We were aiming for something clean, concise, and quirky, and we kept that in mind for both the visual style and how we communicated our message. So many people have different opinions about cottage cheese, we opted to use real interviews, and putting their voices to larger-than-life characters was fun and speaks to the exciting, active lifestyle a health food can facilitate.



Role


This project was completed by a team of three members. To start off, we were all part of the brainstorming process. From ideation to final product, each team member had a say in every step of the process. Being one of the three members, I created and animated two of the six characters and assisted with tweaks on the rest of them.
