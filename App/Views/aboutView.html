Siddhant Shah is a Web and UI Developer and has a passion for creating art in multiple media inlcuding 3D. He also dabbles in game design and development.

Siddhant looks for social narratives in the art he personally curates and believes in the work of Don Hertzfeldt as the zeitgeist. Filmmaking is an outlet for his wandering mind, and he hopes to transform audiences with his eccentric style.
