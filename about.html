Siddhant Shah is a graduate of the Rochester Institute of Technology where he majored in film and animation with a focus in 3D and minored in game design and development.


As an animator, he has worked for Partners and Napier to produce several 10-15 second animated commercials for Friendship Dairies as part of its social media campaign on Facebook, Youtube, and Instagram. He has also worked for The Wedding Filmer in Bombay, India as an assistant video editor and graphic designer.


Siddhant looks for social narratives in the art he personally curates and believes in the work of Don Hertzfeldt as the zeitgeist. Filmmaking is an outlet for his wandering mind, and he hopes to transform audiences with his eccentric style.
